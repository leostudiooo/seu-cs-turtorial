Class Line: double a, b, c to express line a x + b y + c == 0.

Member functions:
1. default constructor
2. constructor with param
3. copy constructor
4. solve the area of the triangle of A(-c/a, 0), B(-c/b, 0) and O(0,0)
5. judge if two lines are parallel (or the same)
6. reload operator++ to move the line 1 unit to the right
7. reload operator>> to input a, b, and c.
8. reload operator<< to output the equation of the line.

Test all the member functions in main().
